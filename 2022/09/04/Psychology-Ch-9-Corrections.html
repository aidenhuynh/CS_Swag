Question:

Which of the following techniques used by professional therapists are highly likely to promote the construction of false memories?

Correct Answer (write it out, not just the letter!):

Hypnosis, imagination-enhancing techniques, and drug-induced recall.

Why the correct answer is right (content based explanation):

All of these techniques can implant false memories. Those with vivid imaginations were found to be more susceptible to “imagination inflation,” where repeatedly imagining nonexistent events creates false memories. Therefore, enhancing imagination would increase the amount of false memories. Drug-induced recall and hypnosis have been criticized heavily by psychologists, to the point of being agreed upon as unreliable by several large organizations.
